28/2/25 - Sid Meier's Pirates!, a forgotten game.

Sid Meier's Pirates is a game released in 2004. It is a pirate simulator, based on the older versions of the game for most 8-bit systems in the 80s. The game focuses on rescuing family members, looting and dancing (can't believe Just Dance copied this masterpiece) The game is forgotten however, and I consider it a "hidden gem". Now of course, you've heard that like a thousand times. If you had one dollar for every game considered a "hidden gem", you could have enough money to buy some of them! However, Pirates! is a game that is ACTUALLY a hidden gem. The graphics may look a bit cartoony and janky, but considering that this game was released in 2004, these graphics look amazing for the time. The music, my god the music. Sometimes it is relaxing, sometimes it prepares you for adventure. It actually sounds like music that could be played on a pirate ship. Lets not ignore the most important factor however: GAMEPLAY! The gameplay consists of sword fighting, looting ships and completing minigames. Those minigames are barely actually considered minigames, because they have a ton of attention put into them, like the land combat, in which you can either go to prison and lose a good chunk of stuff, or you could get a ton of loot from a city. No game is without flaws however. After a while, the game becomes kinda boring and that doesn't pair well with the fact that certain parts of gameplay feel slugish. There will be moments where your ship barely moves, because there is no wind. Another annoying part is the treasure hunting. 90% of the time, you will find the treasure with ease, but sometimes it could take up to half an hour to find it. Overall, I think Pirates is an underlooked gem, that deserves more recognition. RATING: 8.7/10
