Particle Accelerator II

Particle Accelerator II is the sequel to the original Particle Accelerator. It is a recreation of an old flash game, called "Particles". Use the mouse to move the blue ball and try to avoid the red balls. Be careful though, cause more and more of them spawn in as you play.
You might be wondering what the hell is different from the original. Well, there are new balls, gamepad support, extra difficulty and some groovy Kevin Macleod music.
